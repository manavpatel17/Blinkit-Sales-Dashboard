# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Business Intelligence Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze Blinkit's grocery sales performance.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights by evaluating sales performance, outlet efficiency, product categories, customer ratings, and outlet characteristics.

The objective is to help business stakeholders identify growth opportunities and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Compare outlet performance across different locations
- Evaluate product category contribution
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns
- Understand the impact of outlet size and outlet type
- Support strategic business decisions using interactive dashboards

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 🏆 Best Performing Outlet

- Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest sales (**$787.55K**).

---

### 🌍 Outlet Location

- Tier 3 outlets contributed the highest sales (**$472.13K**).

---

### 🥇 Top Product Categories

- Fruits
- Snack Foods

These categories generated the highest revenue.

---

### 🥑 Fat Content Analysis

Regular-fat products generated significantly higher sales compared to Low-fat products.

---

### 📈 Business Performance

The dashboard highlights strong sales concentration within specific outlet types and product categories, enabling management to identify expansion opportunities.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand high-performing Supermarket Type 1 outlets.
- Increase inventory for top-selling product categories.
- Improve performance in underperforming outlet locations.
- Promote Low-fat products through targeted marketing campaigns.
- Optimize inventory planning using sales trends.
